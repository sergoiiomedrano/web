ABOUT SERGIO MEDRANO

Hello, my name is Sergio and I am an Engineer and programming enthusiast.

I studied Aeronautical Engineering at the Autonomous University of Nuevo León, and began my career working at the commercial airline Viva Aerobus, there I worked as Crew Support at the Viva Aerobus Control Center, where I was able to learn about how they connect the different areas of Crews, Dispatch and Maintenance, as well as the pace of work of an airline and the operational repercussions that may exist in its operation. In 2022 I started working in the position of Asset Management Engineer, where I managed the maintenance of the fleet engines.

In 2023, following one of the things that I like, I began to study the technical career of Web Development at UANL. I consider Programming as an extremely useful tool for my professional and personal life, for which I have studied programming languages focused on different applications, front-end such as HTML and Javascript, as well as familiar with data manipulation languages such as Visual Basic, Python and SQL.
